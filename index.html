How Does It Work?

Steve guides kids through the adventure — they supply the moves!

1

Pick an Adventure

Choose a world — Dragon Forest, Spooky Dungeon, Krampus Mountain, and more. Each episode is a fresh quest.

2

Clear some space

Push back the couch, grab a friend or sibling, and get ready to move! No gear needed — just energy.

3

Follow Steve's lead

Steve calls out actions throughout the adventure. Run from the dragon! Duck under the trap! High-five the screen!

4

Save the day 🎉

Every adventure ends in victory. Steve celebrates with the kids and gets them hyped for the next quest.

Meet Steve

Steve is your host, your guide, and your hype man. He shows up every adventure in his iconic yellow-and-black stripes, ready to lead kids through wild worlds and epic quests.

He's goofy. He's energetic. He treats every kid like his actual adventure partner. When you win, Steve wins. When you mess up a jump, Steve laughs and cheers you on anyway.

Steve isn't a teacher — he's the cooler older brother who always knows where the best adventure is hiding.
